## Performance Testing Standards
- Peak Point: Max TPS while p90 meets SLA and error rate < 1%
- Breakpoint: Error rate > 5% or timeout rate > 10%
- Stability: Sustain peak for 1-4 hours with no degradation
- Ramp-up: Stepped (10-20 users/step) to protect MQ/Cassandra
- Transaction mix: Must sum to 100%
